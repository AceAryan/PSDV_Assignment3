Practical Applications of PyMC

1. Medical Diagnosis

PyMC is used in disease prediction by modeling uncertainty in symptoms and test results. Bayesian models help estimate the probability of a disease given observed symptoms.

2. Financial Risk Analysis

In finance, PyMC helps in portfolio risk estimation by modeling stock returns using distributions like Normal or Student-T.

3. A/B Testing in Marketing

Businesses use Bayesian A/B testing with PyMC to analyze conversion rates and optimize marketing campaigns.

4. Manufacturing and Quality Control

PyMC helps detect anomalies in production lines by modeling product defects and process variability.

5. Sports Analytics

Used to predict player performance using Bayesian hierarchical models, improving scouting and game strategies.

6. Environmental Science

PyMC models climate change patterns and estimates uncertainties in weather predictions.
